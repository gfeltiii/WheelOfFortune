Wheel of Fortune Rules

Welcome to Wheel of Fortune! Below are the rules of the game:

  1. Objective: The goal of the game is to guess the letters of a hidden phrase or word before time runs out.
  2. Game Setup: The game consists of a spinning wheel divided into segments. Each segment represents a different cash value or a special prize.
  3. How to Play:
    • The player will take turns spinning the wheel.
    • Each time the wheel stops, the player is given the opportunity to guess a letter in the phrase.
    • If the guessed letter is in the phrase, the player earns the amount indicated on the wheel and the letter will be revealed.
    • If the guessed letter is not in the phrase, the turn is passed to the next player.
  4. Special Segments:
    • Bankrupt: If the wheel lands on "Bankrupt," the player loses all accumulated earnings for that round.
    • Free Play: A "Free Play" allows a player to guess a letter without risking money if the letter is not in the phrase.
  5. Winning: The player who correctly guesses all the letters of the phrase wins the round. The game continues with new phrases until a final winner is declared.
  6. Final Round: In the final round, the player will have a limited amount of time to guess the remaining letters in the phrase. If they succeed, they win the grand prize.

Good luck, and have fun spinning the wheel!
